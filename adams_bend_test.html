Scoliosis Screening Assistant

Based on the Adams Forward Bend Test

Important Disclaimer

This is a screening tool, not a diagnostic tool. It helps identify potential signs that warrant a professional medical evaluation. It does not replace a consultation with a doctor. Always consult a healthcare professional for any medical concerns.

Instructions

For the person being screened:

  • Remove shirt to make the back visible.
  • Stand with feet together and knees straight.
  • Bend forward at the waist, as if touching your toes.
  • Let your back become parallel with the floor.
  • Let your arms hang freely.

For the person taking the picture:

  • Stand directly behind the person.
  • Ensure the area is well-lit.
  • Frame the photo to include the entire back, from the waist to the head.
